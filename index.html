About Me!

Short Bio
My name is Mike, I wrestled around a farm when I was younger, in a small small small central Utah community. I went to Delta High School, yes I was a rabbit. Rabbit’s are plentiful where I am from. They are also well known as smart, grumpy, anal, greedy, and sometimes very unfriendly if you like Winnie the Pooh movies anyway. It doesn’t matter if I like them or not, I have a young child that loves them, so I get to watch them over and over again. I graduated from Delta High School and went down to SUU to take a semester of college where I took a CS class or two as a Thunderbird, they aren’t real. After taking a semester at SUU, I moved back home to wrestle around on a farm again, this time a dairy farm. That lasted about another semester, and I went to UVU where I became a Wolverine. Two semesters passed by and I moved out to Winnemucca NV to work in a Silver Mine, I ended up working for a drilling company. I didn’t like it so much, and I only stayed out there for a month. I moved back to Utah and started school at Utah State University. I fell in love with the atmosphere and how it felt a little bit smaller than any other place I had been to after graduating High School. It took me about 5 years to get a Bachelors Degree in Technical and Professional Writing. I met my wife in the program there, and we got married a few years out of college. I started working at Xactware in 2011; I love that it has values and has been a great company to work for. When the opportunity arose for me to join the program, I jumped at the opportunity to strengthen my skills to become a better software tester and hopefully learn how to be a developer, so I can move up in my company.
